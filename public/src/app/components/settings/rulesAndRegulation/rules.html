Rules & Regulations

  • Follow these simples to play Fantumn’s fantasy football game of skill.

    • Pick a match.
    • Create your own team of 11.
    • Manage your team
    • Gain points
    • Win amazing prizes/gifts and coupons.

  • Live tab shows the current live matches if any.

  • History tab shows the list of all matches, which got completed.

  • Pick a match - Select a match that you want to play from the list available.

  • Creating your own team - You are allowed to pick players on the below combination only:

    • 1 - Goalkeeper.
    • 3 - Forwards (Maximum of 2 from one team and minimum of 1 from the other team).
    • 3 - Midfielders (Maximum of 2 from one team and minimum of 1 from the other team).
    • 4 - Defenders (Maximum of 3 from one team and minimum of 1 from the other team).

  • Manage your team - You can make unlimited changes to your team before the deadline.

  • Gain points - For every match that you play, the point gained from in it will be added to your account.

  • Prizes/Gifts - We are planning to partner up with many businesses to help them brand and as well as provide gifts to the users.
    The workflow of gift store will be updated here as soon as the gifts are loaded and an official communication will be sent to you. For more info, please contact “admin@fantumn.com”.

  • Fantumn, as of now, only offers the english league games.

  • You, as a “User”, can only enter a match once.

  • Upcoming tab shows the list of all upcoming matches for the current game week.

  • Live tab - live leaderboards represent the current rankings of all users, who have joined that particular match.

  • History tab - history leaderboards show the final rankings of all users, who have joined that particular match.

  • Only users above an age of 18 are allowed to participate and claim prizes/gifts or coupons based on their winning.
